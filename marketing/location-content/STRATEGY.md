# Location-Based Organic Content Strategy

## Goal
Maximize organic reach by geotagging content to 15+ cities where shoots have been done. Instagram and TikTok surface content to users browsing location feeds — every post geotagged to a city becomes discoverable by everyone in/searching that city.

## Why This Works
- **Location pages are underused goldmines.** Most photographers only geotag their own city. You have shoots across 19 cities on 4 continents — each one is a separate discovery channel.
- **Multiple posts per day is fine** if each post targets a different city's location feed. You're not spamming one audience — you're showing up in 15+ separate local feeds.
- **Film photography + editorial look stands out** in location feeds dominated by phone snapshots and tourist photos.

## Posting Cadence
- **3x/day** across Instagram Reels, TikTok, and IG carousels
- Rotate cities so the same location feed doesn't see back-to-back posts
- Best times: 7-9am, 12-2pm, 6-9pm local time for each target city

## Content Types (ranked by engagement potential)

### 1. City Reveal Reels (highest reach)
- Quick slideshow of 4-6 photos from one city
- Text overlay: "Shot on 35mm film in [City]"
- Trending audio + geotag to that city
- 8-15 seconds, fast cuts

### 2. Before/Direction Reels (high engagement)
- "How I direct someone who's never modeled"
- Show 2-3 frames progressing from awkward to confident
- Educational + aspirational = saves + shares

### 3. Single Hero Image Posts (steady reach)
- One striking image, strong caption with location storytelling
- Geotag + location hashtags
- These build the grid aesthetic

### 4. Carousel Deep Dives (high saves)
- 5-10 images from one shoot
- Caption tells the story of shooting in that city
- "Swipe through [Name]'s session in [City]"

### 5. POV/Process Reels (shareability)
- "POV: you booked a film shoot in Tokyo"
- Walk-through of the experience
- Drives bookings directly

## Hashtag Strategy (per post, max 5-8)
- 1 city-specific: #TokyoPhotographer, #RomePortraits
- 1 country-specific: #JapanPhotography, #ItalyPortraits
- 1 medium: #35mmFilm or #ShotOnFilm
- 1 style: #EditorialPortrait or #FilmPortrait
- 1 broad: #PortraitPhotography
- 1 niche: #FilmIsNotDead or #AnalogPortrait

## City Priority Tiers

### Tier 1 — Active Markets (post daily)
- Manila, Bangkok, Bali/Canggu, Tokyo

### Tier 2 — Portfolio Showcase (3-4x/week)
- Rome, Venice, Vienna, Da Nang, Saigon

### Tier 3 — Long-tail Discovery (2-3x/week)
- Berlin, Warsaw, Krakow, Glasgow, Cascais, Sitges, Ghent, Bratislava, Milan, Dunedin, Rotorua

## Key Metrics to Track
- Reach from Explore/Location (not followers)
- Profile visits from non-followers
- Saves (indicates high-value content)
- DM inquiries per city
